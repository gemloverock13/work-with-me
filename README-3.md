# Work With Me — Brain in Hand

A neuroinclusive working preferences profile builder. Helps employees communicate how they work best — without requiring medical disclosure, diagnosis, or blank-page writing.

## What this is

**Work With Me** is a tile-based tool that lets people build a working preferences profile they can share with colleagues. It covers:

- Communication preferences (remote-first)
- Energy — what charges and what drains
- Social context (the things that cost energy but rarely get named)
- Meeting preferences
- Feedback and information processing
- Energy through the day (time-of-day patterns)
- Things colleagues should know

Every item has three states: **on my profile**, **I know — not shared** (private reflection only), or **I'd rather not say**. People control exactly what appears publicly.

A consent gate appears before anything is published, with clear language about what the profile is and is not.

## What this is not

This tool is not:
- A medical assessment or diagnostic tool
- A formal reasonable adjustment request
- A legal document
- A replacement for Access to Work, Occupational Health, or any clinical support

It is designed to sit **alongside** those processes, not replace them. The tool does not create obligations on any party.

## Design principles

- **Tile-based, not text-based** — reduces blank-page anxiety, especially for ND individuals
- **Third-person avatar framing** — helps people think about themselves without it feeling like a confession
- **No defaults that look like disclosure** — nothing is set to "public" by default
- **Remote-first language** — written for distributed teams
- **Consent before publish** — explicit agreement required before anything goes on a profile
- **Legal care** — avoids language about "formal" or "informal" requests; points to existing organisational processes
